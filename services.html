Modern Medicine Services (Dr. Dhirendra Yadav)

General Physician Consultation

Our general physician services cover a wide range of acute and chronic health issues. We provide initial diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing management for common illnesses, infections, and general health concerns.

  • Routine check-ups and preventive care
  • Management of acute illnesses (fever, flu, infections)
  • Health screenings and vaccinations
  • Referrals to specialists when necessary

Diabetology & Metabolic Disorders

Specialized care for diabetes management, including Type 1, Type 2, and gestational diabetes. Our approach focuses on blood sugar control, preventing complications, and improving quality of life through medication, diet, and lifestyle modifications.

  • Personalized diabetes management plans
  • Monitoring and control of blood glucose levels
  • Prevention and management of diabetic complications
  • Nutritional counseling and lifestyle guidance

Cardiac Care & Hypertension Management

Comprehensive care for cardiovascular health, including diagnosis and management of hypertension (high blood pressure), cholesterol disorders, and other cardiac risk factors. We emphasize preventive strategies and early intervention.

  • Blood pressure monitoring and control
  • Cholesterol management
  • Risk assessment for heart disease
  • Lifestyle interventions for cardiac health

Renal Care & Kidney Health

Diagnosis and management of various kidney conditions, including chronic kidney disease, kidney stones, and urinary tract infections. Our goal is to preserve kidney function and prevent progression of renal disorders.

  • Early detection of kidney disease
  • Management of chronic kidney disease
  • Dietary advice for kidney health
  • Treatment of urinary tract infections
