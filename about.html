About Saanvi Healthcare Centre

Where Modern Medicine meets the Healing Wisdom of Ayurveda

Our Story & Mission

Saanvi Healthcare Centre was founded with a vision to bring together the best of both worlds — the precision of modern medicine and the time-tested wisdom of Ayurveda. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, compassionate, and personalized healthcare under one roof.

We believe true healing comes from understanding the root cause of illness, not just treating symptoms. With a unique integrative approach, we aim to restore balance, vitality, and long-term wellness in every patient we serve.

At Saanvi Healthcare Centre, patients experience advanced diagnostics, preventive care, Ayurvedic therapies, and holistic guidance — all guided by experienced specialists.

Meet Our Doctors

Dr. Dhirendra Yadav

MBBS, MD (Internal Medicine), CCDM

Consultant Physician & Diabetologist

With over 19 years of rich experience, Dr. Dhirendra Yadav is a trusted name in internal medicine and diabetes management. His expertise spans chronic diseases, infections, metabolic disorders, and preventive health. Known for his empathetic approach and diagnostic precision, he ensures that every patient receives scientifically sound and individualized care.

His mission is to empower patients with knowledge and guide them toward a healthier, more balanced life through evidence-based medicine, lifestyle correction, and continuous follow-up.

Dr. Sunitha Yadav

BAMS, MD (Ayurveda Medicine)

Consultant Ayurveda Physician

Dr. Sunitha Yadav is a dedicated Ayurveda expert with 14 years of professional practice in holistic and traditional healing. She specializes in lifestyle diseases, skin and joint disorders, women’s health, and stress management.

Her treatment philosophy revolves around balancing the body’s natural energies — Vata, Pitta, and Kapha — and promoting wellness through herbal therapies, detoxification, and mindful living.

Dr. Sunitha believes that Ayurveda is not merely a system of medicine but a way of life — guiding people toward harmony of body, mind, and soul.
